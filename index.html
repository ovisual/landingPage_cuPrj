DIGITAL SKILLS FOR DIGITAL ECONOMY

Register for the Ota Youth Initiative ICT Program organized by Covenant University in collaboration with UNITeS Cisco.

The program aims to equip 200 Ota youth residents with the essential digital skills required to thrive in a changing economy and promote digital transformation in our society.

This "Youth Empowerment Initiative" is a mutually agreed plan for implementing agency, community members, and other stakeholders. The program is highly beneficial not only for the youth of the community but for the community as a whole. It envisages the development of the community, as the project has the capacity to turn the non-productive youth of the community into responsible citizens who will work towards the holistic development of themselves and society.

The program will run for three weeks, starting from Thursday, 27th July - Saturday, 19th August 2023, with daily sessions from 9:00 am to 4:00 pm. The training will be held at the Covenant University lecture theatre, and participants will be housed in the university hostel for the duration of the program.

The program is completely free of charge.
